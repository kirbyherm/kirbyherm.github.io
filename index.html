I am a Nuclear Astrophysics Ph.D. candidate at Michigan State University supported by the Joint Institute for Nuclear Astrophysics -- Center for the Evolution of the Elements.

My research interests include Urca cooling in superbursting systems, and nucleosynthesis in core-collape supernovae.

A list of my scientific publications can be found here. For more information, please see my CV.

Additionally, I am very interested in energy and climate policy, and ensuring responsible stewardship of nuclear weapons and waste.
