I am a Nuclear Astrophysics Ph.D. candidate at Michigan State University supported by the Joint Institute for Nuclear Astrophysics -- Center for the Evolution of the Elements. My expected graduation is Fall 2023 and I am interested in both industry and academic opportunities. I love collecting, cleaning, and modeling data and enjoy finding ways to put that skill set to good use.

My research interests include Urca cooling in superbursting systems, nucleosynthesis in core-collape supernovae, and optimizing accelerator methods for nuclear astrophysics experiments.
